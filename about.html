About Made CRM

Built by someone who
actually uses a CRM.

Made CRM started with a simple observation: most small B2B companies are either paying too much for a CRM they barely use, or struggling with spreadsheets that don't scale.

I built my first custom CRM for a small team that had outgrown their spreadsheets but found their current CRM overwhelming, overpriced, and full of features they weren't utilizing. The result was a purpose-built tool that fit their exact workflow — their pipeline stages, their team, their terminology — and cost a fraction of what they were previously paying.

The experience made one thing clear: small B2B teams don't need enterprise software. They need something built for them, delivered by someone who understands their business, and maintained by someone they can actually reach.

That's what Made CRM is. We build custom CRM systems for small teams — biotech, financial advisory, consulting, agencies — that are simple enough to use on day one and powerful enough to grow with your business.
