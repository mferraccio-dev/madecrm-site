About Made CRM

Built by someone who actually uses a CRM.

A one-person studio building purpose-fit CRMs for small B2B teams — biotech, advisory, consulting, agencies. Less a SaaS product, more a craftsman with a workshop.

The story

Made CRM started with a simple observation: most small B2B companies are either paying too much for a CRM they barely use, or struggling with spreadsheets that don't scale.

I built my first custom CRM for a small team that had outgrown their spreadsheets but found their existing CRM overwhelming, overpriced, and full of features they weren't using. The result was a purpose-built tool that fit their exact workflow — their pipeline stages, their team, their terminology — and cost a fraction of what they were previously paying.

Small B2B teams don't need enterprise software. They need something built for them.

The experience made one thing clear. They need a tool delivered by someone who understands their business, and maintained by someone they can actually reach.

That's what Made CRM is. Custom CRM systems for small teams — biotech, financial advisory, consulting, agencies — simple enough to use on day one, built to grow with your business, and looked after by the same person who built it.

What makes us different

Four principles. Held tightly.

Every CRM we build runs on the same set of beliefs about what small teams actually need.

Built for your workflow

Every CRM starts with a conversation about how you work. Your stages, your fields, your team names — not a generic template, not a pre-built questionnaire.

A person, not a ticket

When something needs fixing, you reach the person who built your system — not a support queue. We stay on retainer for a reason.

Your data is yours

Each client gets their own isolated database. Your data is never shared, and you can export it or take the codebase at any time.

Fast delivery

Most CRMs are live within 2–4 weeks. No months-long implementation projects, no consultants, no complexity for its own sake.
