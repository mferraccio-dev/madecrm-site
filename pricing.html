What affects the quote

Every project is different. These are the factors that typically affect scope — and what we talk through on the discovery call.

Team size

More users means more logins, more owner filters, and more nuance in how reports are broken out by person.

Pipeline complexity

A single pipeline with 5 stages is straightforward. Multiple pipelines with custom fields, deal types, and conditional logic takes more time.

Data migration

Clean spreadsheets migrate quickly. Multiple sources, duplicate records, or messy historical data requires more cleanup work.

Custom features

Standard builds cover pipeline, contacts, companies, activity, and reports. Custom fields, integrations, or unique workflows are scoped separately.

Industry fit

Some industries have specific terminology, deal structures, or reporting needs that require more configuration out of the gate.

Timeline

Standard delivery is under 2 weeks. Tighter rush deadlines can be accommodated and may affect scope.

Common questions
Why don't you list specific prices?
Because a 3-person consulting firm and a 12-person biotech team need very different things. Listing a single price either undersells what we do or prices out teams that would be a great fit. A 30-minute call tells us everything we need to give you a real number.
How much will it cost?
Setup is $5,000 for a standard build — flat, no hourly billing. Monthly retainer is $400. Larger or more complex scopes (multiple custom pipelines, deep integrations, heavy data migration) are quoted on the discovery call — but always flat, never by the hour.
Is there a per-seat fee?
No. We don't charge per user. Your retainer covers your entire team — whether that's 3 people or 15.
What happens if I want to cancel?
Cancel the retainer with 30 days written notice. We provide a full data export within 5 business days. Your data is always yours.
