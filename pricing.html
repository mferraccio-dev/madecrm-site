Common questions

How long does it take to build?
Most projects are live within 2–4 weeks from kickoff. The timeline depends on how quickly we receive your data and feedback.
What happens if I cancel?
Cancel the retainer with 30 days written notice. We'll provide a full data export within 5 business days. You own your data — always.
Can I migrate my data from HubSpot or another CRM?
Yes — data migration from one source is included in the setup fee. Additional sources (a second CRM, multiple spreadsheets) are available as an add-on.
Is there a per-user fee?
No. The retainer covers your entire team — up to 10 users included, with additional users available on request.
What if I need features that aren't in the standard build?
Custom feature development is available at $125/hour, scoped and agreed upon before work begins. Nothing surprises you.
