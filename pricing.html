Common questions

How long does it take to build?
Most projects are live within 2–4 weeks from kickoff. Timeline depends on how quickly we receive your data and feedback during the review phase.
What happens if I cancel?
Cancel the retainer with 30 days written notice. We'll provide a full data export within 5 business days and permanently delete your data from our servers within 30 days. You own your data — always.
Can I migrate from HubSpot or Salesforce?
Yes — migration from one source is included in the setup fee. Additional sources are available as an add-on, scoped based on complexity.
Is there a per-user fee?
No. The retainer covers your entire team — up to 10 users included, with additional users available on request at no extra cost.
What if I need features that aren't in the standard build?
Custom feature development is available at $125/hour, scoped and agreed upon before work begins. No surprise charges.
Do I own the code?
Yes. Your CRM lives in a dedicated GitHub repository that belongs to you. You can self-host or take the codebase at any time.
